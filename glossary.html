Crypto Glossary

Every term you need to know to confidently build and launch your branded crypto currency.

ABCDEFGLMNPSTW
A
Airdrop Marketing
A distribution of free tokens to a list of wallet addresses, typically used to build an early holder base and generate awareness before or after a coin launch.
Anti-Whale Mechanism Tokenomics
A smart contract rule that limits the maximum number of tokens any single wallet can hold or trade at one time, preventing large holders from manipulating the price.
Audit Security
A professional security review of a smart contract's code, conducted to identify vulnerabilities before deployment. Audited contracts build trust with potential holders and exchanges.
B
Blockchain Infrastructure
A decentralized, distributed digital ledger that records all transactions across a network of computers. Your branded crypto currency lives and operates on a blockchain.
BEP-20 Standard
The token standard for the BNB Smart Chain (formerly Binance Smart Chain). BEP-20 tokens are faster and cheaper to deploy than Ethereum-based tokens.
Burn Tokenomics
The permanent removal of tokens from circulation, typically by sending them to an unrecoverable wallet address. Burning reduces supply and can increase scarcity over time.
C
CoinGecko Exchange
One of the world's largest cryptocurrency data aggregators. Getting listed on CoinGecko gives your currency public visibility, a price chart, and credibility with potential holders.
CoinMarketCap Exchange
The most widely visited cryptocurrency data platform. A CoinMarketCap listing is considered a major milestone for any new currency launch.
Contract Address Technical
The unique identifier assigned to your smart contract when it is deployed on a blockchain. This address is what exchanges and holders use to find and interact with your currency.
D
DEX (Decentralized Exchange) Exchange
A peer-to-peer trading platform that allows users to trade cryptocurrencies directly from their wallets without a central authority. Examples include Uniswap and PancakeSwap.
DEXTools Exchange
A popular analytics platform for decentralized exchanges. Getting listed on DEXTools provides real-time price charts and trading data for your currency.
Distribution Tokenomics
The plan for how your total token supply is allocated across categories such as liquidity pool, team allocation, marketing, community rewards, and public sale.
E
ERC-20 Standard
The most widely used token standard on the Ethereum blockchain. ERC-20 tokens are compatible with the largest ecosystem of wallets, exchanges, and DeFi applications.
F
Fee / Tax Tokenomics
A percentage charged on each buy or sell transaction, built into the smart contract. Fees are typically split between liquidity, marketing, and burn functions.
Fiat General
Traditional government-issued currency such as US dollars or euros, as opposed to cryptocurrency.
G
Gas Fee Technical
The transaction fee paid to the blockchain network to process and confirm a transaction. On Ethereum, gas fees can be high during periods of network congestion.
L
Liquidity Pool (LP) Technical
A pool of tokens locked in a smart contract that enables trading on a decentralized exchange. Adding liquidity to your currency makes it tradeable on platforms like Uniswap.
LP Lock Security
The process of locking liquidity pool tokens in a time-locked contract so the creator cannot remove liquidity and abandon the project. LP locking builds trust with holders.
M
Mainnet Technical
The live, real blockchain network where actual transactions occur with real value. Contrasts with testnet, which is used for testing only.
Market Cap General
The total market value of a cryptocurrency, calculated by multiplying the current price by the circulating supply.
Mint Technical
The process of creating new tokens on a blockchain. When you deploy your smart contract, you mint your initial token supply.
N
NFT (Non-Fungible Token) General
A unique digital asset on a blockchain that cannot be replicated or exchanged on a one-to-one basis. Different from standard crypto currencies which are fungible.
P
PancakeSwap Exchange
The leading decentralized exchange on the BNB Smart Chain. Ideal for listing BEP-20 branded crypto currencies due to low fees and high volume.
Presale Launch
A period before the public launch when early supporters can purchase tokens at a discounted rate. Presales help raise initial liquidity and build a committed holder base.
S
Smart Contract Technical
Self-executing code stored on a blockchain that automatically enforces the rules of your token — including supply, taxes, burn mechanics, and trading restrictions.
SPL Token Standard
The token standard for the Solana blockchain. SPL tokens benefit from Solana's extremely fast transaction speeds and very low fees.
Supply Tokenomics
The total number of tokens that exist for your currency. Total supply includes all tokens ever created; circulating supply refers to tokens currently available to the public.
T
Ticker Symbol General
The short abbreviation used to identify your currency on exchanges and charts. For example, DOGE, SHIB, or PEPE. Your ticker is set when you deploy your smart contract.
Tokenomics Tokenomics
The economic model of your crypto currency — covering total supply, distribution, burn schedule, transaction taxes, and vesting periods. Strong tokenomics are critical to long-term value.
Testnet Technical
A simulated blockchain environment used for testing smart contracts without using real money. Always test your contract on a testnet before deploying to mainnet.
W
Wallet Technical
A digital application that stores your private keys and allows you to send, receive, and manage cryptocurrency. Common wallets include MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and Phantom.
Whitepaper Launch
A formal document that describes your currency's purpose, tokenomics, roadmap, and team. A professional whitepaper is typically required for exchange listings and builds credibility with potential holders.
