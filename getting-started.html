Your Side-by-Side Roadmap

Every step on the left has an equivalent you already understand on the right.

🪙 Crypto Launch
🛒 Online Store
Step 1
👛
Get a Crypto Wallet
MetaMask is your digital wallet — think of it like a purse or billfold that lives on your phone or computer. It holds your crypto (digital money), approves your actions on the network, and gives you a unique identity on the blockchain (the giant public record book where all crypto activity is permanently tracked). Free to download, takes 5 minutes to set up.
MetaMask — Free
1
Equivalent
🏦
Open a PayPal or Bank Account
Before you can sell anything online you need somewhere to receive and hold money. A PayPal or Stripe account is your financial identity online — nobody can pay you without it. Same idea, different technology.
PayPal / Stripe — Free
Step 2
💵
Fund Your Wallet
Buy $50–$100 of ETH or BNB (these are types of cryptocurrency — think of them like different currencies in different countries) on Kraken (a trusted online exchange, like a currency exchange desk at an airport). Send it to your MetaMask wallet to cover gas fees (small processing charges the network collects every time you do something — just like a transaction fee at a bank). This is your one-time cost to get started.
Kraken — ~$50–$100
2
Equivalent
💳
Buy Your Starting Inventory
Every online store needs startup capital — money to buy your first batch of products before you can sell them. This is your cost of entry into business. In crypto, you're funding the system instead of a warehouse.
Wholesale / Alibaba — ~$100–$500
Step 3
⛓️
Choose Your Blockchain
A blockchain (think of it as a giant public spreadsheet that records every transaction and can never be erased or tampered with) is the network your coin will live on. You need to pick one — Ethereum, BNB Chain, or Solana. Each is like a different country with its own rules and costs. We recommend BNB Chain for beginners — it has the lowest fees, the easiest setup, and your MetaMask wallet works with it automatically.
BNB Chain — Recommended
3
Equivalent
🏪
Choose Your Platform
Shopify, Etsy, or Amazon — you have to decide where your store lives. Each platform has different costs, audiences, and rules. Shopify is the most popular choice for beginners because it's beginner-friendly and gives you the most control. Same decision, same logic.
Shopify — Recommended
Step 4
🪙
Create Your Coin
Use Token Tool — a website that acts like a coin-building wizard. No coding or technical knowledge needed. Fill in your coin name, ticker symbol (the short abbreviation for your coin — like DOGE or BTC), and total supply (how many coins will ever exist). Pay a small gas fee (processing charge) and within minutes your coin officially exists on the blockchain forever. You'll receive a contract address — a permanent unique ID for your coin, like a serial number. Save it immediately.
Token Tool — ~$20–$50 gas
4
Equivalent
📦
List Your Product
Creating your product listing on Shopify or Etsy — adding photos, a title, description, and a price. This is the moment your product officially exists in the marketplace and can be seen by the world. Your coin's creation is the exact same moment.
Shopify / Etsy — Free to list
Step 5
💧
Add Liquidity So Your Coin Can Be Traded
Your coin exists but nobody can buy or sell it yet. Adding liquidity (think of it like stocking a store shelf — putting your product out so customers can actually purchase it) is done through a DEX (Decentralized Exchange — an online marketplace where people trade crypto directly with each other, with no bank or middleman involved). You deposit some of your coins alongside some ETH or BNB and that combination is what lets anyone in the world trade your coin. The more you deposit, the more stable your price will be.
PancakeSwap — ~$50–$200
5
Equivalent
🚪
Open Your Store for Business
Hitting "publish" and making your store live so customers can actually check out and pay. Until you do this, nobody can buy anything. Adding liquidity is the crypto version of flipping the open sign on your door for the very first time.
Shopify — $29/mo
Step 6
📋
Get Listed on CoinGecko & CoinMarketCap
CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap (often called CMC) are the two biggest crypto tracking websites in the world — think of them like Google for cryptocurrency. Millions of investors search these sites every single day looking for new coins to buy. Getting your coin listed here gives it instant credibility and makes it searchable worldwide. You'll need your contract address (your coin's unique ID from Step 4), your logo, and your website. Both listings are completely free.
CoinGecko / CMC — Free
6
Equivalent
🔍
Get Listed on Google Shopping
Submitting your products to Google Shopping and price comparison sites so buyers who are already searching can discover your store. Free visibility in front of millions of active shoppers. The reach is enormous and it costs nothing to get listed.
Google Merchant — Free
Step 7
👥
Build Your Community
A coin without a community is just a number on a screen. Launch your Twitter/X account, your Telegram group (a community chat app popular in crypto — think of it like a group text with thousands of people), and your Discord server (another community platform with organized channels for different topics). Post updates daily, respond to questions, and make people feel like they're part of something. A strong community is the single biggest driver of a coin's success — more than any ad you'll ever run.
Twitter / Telegram / Discord — Free
7
Equivalent
📱
Build Your Social Following
Create your brand Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook pages. Post product photos and behind-the-scenes content, engage with your followers, and build an audience that trusts your brand. Same idea — community and trust drive sales more than any paid ad ever will.
Instagram / TikTok — Free
Step 8
📣
Promote Your Coin
Reach out to crypto influencers (content creators with large followings in the crypto space) on YouTube and Twitter/X for paid shoutouts or reviews. Post in Reddit communities like r/CryptoMoonShots where investors actively look for new coins. Submit press releases (official news announcements) to crypto news sites like CoinTelegraph. Get listed on CoinHunt and Coinsniper — free sites where new coins are voted on daily by the community. Your MintedLegacy vault has templates ready for all of this.
Reddit / Influencers / PR
8
Equivalent
🎯
Run Ads & Influencer Campaigns
Run Facebook and Instagram ads, partner with influencers in your product niche, and pitch your product to bloggers and online press. Every successful e-commerce brand uses exactly this playbook at launch. Same strategy, different product category.
Meta Ads / Influencers
Step 9
🎁
Launch Your Airdrop (Free Coin Giveaway)
An airdrop means giving away a small number of your coins completely free in exchange for simple actions — like following your Twitter/X account, joining your Telegram group (community chat), or sharing your announcement. People receive the free coins sent directly to their wallet (digital purse). It costs you nothing but a small slice of your total supply. In return you get real holders (people who now own your coin and want it to succeed), real followers, and viral sharing almost overnight. People who receive free coins become your most loyal fans because they have a personal stake in your success.
Your Vault Template — Ready Now
9
Equivalent
🎉
Run a Free Product Giveaway
Give away your product free in exchange for follows, shares, and email signups. Every successful e-commerce brand runs a launch giveaway — it's the fastest and cheapest way to build an audience, create buzz, and get your first wave of loyal customers all at once.
Gleam / KingSumo — Free tier
Step 10
📈
Keep the Momentum Going
Launching your coin is just the beginning. Post weekly updates so your community (your holders — the people who own your coin) knows the project is alive and growing. Hit your roadmap milestones (the public goals you promised your community). Keep adding liquidity (more money deposited into your trading pool) as you grow — it stabilizes price and builds investor confidence. Pursue CEX listings (Centralized Exchanges — larger mainstream trading platforms like Coinbase or Binance where even bigger audiences can discover and buy your coin). Silence kills coins. Consistency wins every time.
Long Game — Never Stop
10
Equivalent
🔄
Scale, Restock & Grow
Add new products, run seasonal sales, grow your email list, expand to new platforms, and keep serving your customers better every month. The most successful online stores never stand still — they compound their growth one consistent action at a time. Your coin is no different.
Long Game — Never Stop
