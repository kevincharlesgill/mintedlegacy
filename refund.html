Refund Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026  ·  Minted Legacy Ltd

We stand behind our educational content and want every member to feel confident subscribing. Please read this policy carefully so you understand your rights and options before purchasing.

Our Commitment

MintedLegacy is a digital subscription service providing educational content, downloadable templates, and community access. Because our core deliverables are digital goods and services that are made available immediately upon subscription, our refund policy reflects the nature of digital content delivery.

7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscribers. If you subscribe to any MintedLegacy plan and are not satisfied within the first 7 days of your initial subscription, you may request a full refund of your first month's payment.

To qualify for the 7-day guarantee:

  • Your refund request must be submitted within 7 days of your initial subscription date
  • This applies to first-time subscribers only — not renewals or re-subscriptions
  • Submit your request by email to refunds@mintedlegacy.com with your account email and reason for the request

Approved refunds will be returned to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Monthly Renewal Charges

After the initial 7-day period, all monthly subscription charges are non-refundable. This is because:

  • Digital content and module access is delivered immediately at the start of each billing cycle
  • Downloadable assets are made available in full at the time of billing
  • Community access and Q&A sessions are ongoing services consumed throughout the month

We encourage all members to cancel their subscription before the next billing date if they no longer wish to continue. You can cancel at any time from your member dashboard and your access will remain active until the end of the current paid period.

How to Cancel

Cancelling your MintedLegacy subscription is simple and can be done at any time:

  • Log into your member dashboard
  • Navigate to Account Settings
  • Select Cancel Subscription
  • Confirm cancellation

Upon cancellation your account remains active until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation is confirmed. We do not offer prorated refunds for the remaining days in a cancelled billing period.

Exceptions & Special Circumstances

We review refund requests outside of our standard policy on a case-by-case basis in the following situations:

  • Duplicate charges or billing errors caused by our systems
  • Technical issues that prevented access to purchased content for an extended period
  • Unauthorized charges resulting from account compromise (please contact us immediately)

We do not issue refunds based on dissatisfaction with cryptocurrency market performance, the outcome of a coin launch, exchange listing decisions, or any external factors outside our control.

Launch Ready Plan — Consultation Calls

One-on-one consultation calls included in the Launch Ready plan are scheduled in advance. If you need to cancel a scheduled call, please provide at least 24 hours notice to reschedule.

Missed calls without 24-hour notice are forfeited for that billing month and cannot be rolled over or refunded individually.

Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. We are committed to resolving any billing issues quickly and fairly. Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in immediate account suspension and could affect your ability to subscribe in the future.

Questions About a Charge?

We're here to help. Reach out to our support team and we'll resolve any billing issue promptly.

Email: refunds@mintedlegacy.com

Please include your account email address and a brief description of your concern and we will respond within 2 business days.
