Community Standards

MintedLegacy is a professional educational community. All members are expected to uphold these standards.

1

Be Respectful

Treat every member with professionalism and courtesy. Personal attacks, harassment, and abusive language will result in immediate removal.

2

No Financial Advice

Do not give or solicit financial investment advice. Share experiences and knowledge but never tell others what to buy, sell, or invest in.

3

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Unsolicited promotion of external services, affiliate links, or unrelated projects outside the designated channels is not permitted.

4

No Scams or Fraud

Any member found promoting fraudulent projects or attempting to deceive other members will be permanently banned and reported.
